# Case Study: 1X NEO Home Robot Development

## Summary

1X Technologies (formerly Halodi Robotics) is developing NEO, a humanoid robot designed for home assistance. Following their earlier EVE platform deployed in security applications, NEO targets the consumer market with a focus on safe human cohabitation.

## Design Philosophy

1. **Safety First**: Designed for homes with people
2. **Affordability**: Target price under $30,000
3. **Human-like Movement**: Natural, non-threatening motion

## Technical Approach

- Compliant actuators for safe interaction
- Soft exterior materials
- Deliberate, human-paced movement
