# Case Study: Stanford Interactive and Safe Robotics

## Summary

Stanford's IRIS lab focuses on human-robot interaction, shared autonomy, and safe robot learning, contributing fundamental algorithms for robots operating around people.

## Key Contributions

1. **Shared Autonomy**: Blending human and robot control
2. **Safe Learning**: Constrained reinforcement learning
3. **Intent Prediction**: Anticipating human behavior
