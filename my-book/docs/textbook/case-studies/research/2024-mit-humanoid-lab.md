# Case Study: MIT Dynamic Locomotion Research

## Summary

MIT's Biomimetic Robotics Lab has pioneered dynamic locomotion research with platforms like Mini Cheetah and the MIT Humanoid. Their work on model predictive control, reinforcement learning for locomotion, and robust dynamic movement has influenced the entire field of legged robotics.

## Key Contributions

1. **Convex MPC for Locomotion**: Real-time optimization for dynamic walking
2. **Learning-based Control**: RL policies for robust locomotion
3. **Hardware Co-design**: Proprioceptive actuators enabling dynamic movement
