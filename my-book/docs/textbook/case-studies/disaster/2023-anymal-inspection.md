# Case Study: ANYmal Industrial Inspection

## Summary

ANYmal, a quadruped robot from Swiss company ANYbotics, has achieved commercial deployment for inspection tasks in hazardous industrial environments including offshore oil platforms, chemical plants, and mining operations.

## Key Applications

1. **Offshore Platforms**: Routine inspection in dangerous environments
2. **Chemical Plants**: Hazardous area monitoring
3. **Mining**: Underground inspection

## Relevance to Humanoids

While ANYmal is a quadruped, the lessons in:
- Robust locomotion
- Industrial deployment
- Safety certification

...directly apply to humanoid development.
