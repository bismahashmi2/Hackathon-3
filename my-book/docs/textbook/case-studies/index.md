## Case Study Format

Each case study follows a consistent structure:

1. **Summary**: Brief overview (200-300 words)
2. **Background**: Company and context information
3. **Technical Implementation**: Detailed technical analysis
4. **Outcomes**: Results and lessons learned
5. **Ethical Considerations**: Relevant ethical aspects
6. **Discussion Questions**: For classroom use
7. **Related Modules**: Links to textbook content
8. **External References**: Further reading
