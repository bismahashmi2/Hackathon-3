# Assessment Package: Module 12

## Components
- Theory Quiz: 15% (HRI principles, safety standards, intent recognition)
- Lab Exercises: 35% (Safety zones, collaboration, NL interface)
- HRI Project: 35% (Design and test collaborative system)
- Ethics Discussion: 15% (Autonomy and safety in care robotics)

## Project: Collaborative Assembly
Design system where robot and human assemble object together. Robot adapts to human pace and provides assistance as needed.

## Quiz Sample
**Q**: Why is motion legibility important in HRI?
**A**: Helps humans predict robot actions, reducing anxiety and enabling effective collaboration
